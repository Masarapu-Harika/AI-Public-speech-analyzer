## üéØ Problem Statement

### The Communication Crisis
In today's digital-first world, effective communication skills are more critical than ever, yet millions of people struggle with:

- **Interview Anxiety**: 73% of job seekers report anxiety during interviews, often due to lack of feedback on their speaking performance
- **Public Speaking Fear**: 75% of the population suffers from glossophobia (fear of public speaking)
- **Professional Communication Gaps**: Remote work has made verbal communication skills even more crucial, but traditional training is expensive and inaccessible
- **Lack of Objective Feedback**: Most people never receive detailed, actionable feedback on their speaking patterns, pace, or emotional delivery
- **Limited Practice Opportunities**: Traditional speech coaching is expensive ($100-300/hour) and not scalable

### Real-World Impact
- Students struggle with presentations and oral exams
- Job seekers fail interviews due to poor communication skills
- Professionals miss promotions due to ineffective speaking abilities
- Non-native speakers lack tools to improve their communication confidence
